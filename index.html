over view

This site is considered an easy and fast way to learn anything related to the automatic Al-haib curriculum and information technology from the first year of middle school to the third secondary school, as the site contains (lessons and curriculum and also programming languages ​​for these classes) explaining lessons in the form of videos and there is also a library of codes for languages They are studied in these stages, and the site also provides a service to communicate with us through the (Contact Us) page on the site.

Our mission

Our mission is to change students' perception of Computer Science, and make them aware of its importance and destroy the idea: that it is an unimportant and difficult subject, which results in students neglecting it, as well as facilitating and simplifying information by explaining in short videos; and facilitating the creation of codes for any programming language specific to the curriculum from the first year of middle school to the third grade Secondary.

Our vision

Our vision is for this platform to achieve the maximum benefit for students in the subject of computer and information technology by explaining the lessons in a simple way; and also one of our most important goals, is to teach programming to students in a broad way; and we put the cornerstone on this through the code library. we aspire to provide a set of courses for the next period of amendments to Platform.
